About Us

Shifaa HomeCare: Professional Home Care for Every Need

Founded in 2023, Shifaa Nursing HomeCare began with the vision to provide compassionate and professional home nursing services for people in need, including the elderly, patients recovering from surgery, and individuals with chronic or terminal illnesses. We continue to lead the way in home healthcare in Bangladesh, with a focus on promoting well-being and improving the quality of life.

Our mission is to provide affordable, high-quality care for patients in the comfort of their homes, ensuring faster recovery, reduced infection risks, and minimal hospital readmissions. From essential daily living assistance to specialized medical and nursing care, we offer a wide range of services tailored to meet the unique needs of each patient.

Professional medical and nursing care in the comfort of your home.

Specialized care for post-surgery recovery, chronic illness management, and more.

Enhancing quality of life through personalized and compassionate care plans.
